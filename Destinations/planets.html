This is Mars it is Arid, rocky, cold and apparently lifeless, the Red Planet offers few hospitalities.

"I Want To Go to Mars some day"-Jermy H.

This is Neptune it is an Ice Gant with 14 Moons. The length of the year is 164.81 earth years.

This is Pluto. Pluto is a complex world of ice mountains and frozen plains. Once considered the ninth planet, Pluto is the largest member of the Kuiper Belt and the best known of a new class of worlds called dwarf planets.

Im Glad Pluto Is Recognized As A Planet!-Kyla Hawkins

Uranus—seventh planet from the Sun—rotates at a nearly 90-degree angle from the plane of its orbit. This unique tilt makes Uranus appear to spin on its side.

This is Venus. Venus is the second planet from the Sun and our closest planetary neighbor. Similar in structure and size to Earth, Venus spins slowly in the opposite direction from most planets. Its thick atmosphere traps heat in a runaway greenhouse effect, making it the hottest planet in our solar system with surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead. Glimpses below the clouds reveal volcanoes and deformed mountains.
